about us

Welcome to Taspeeh Keeper, your go-to online platform dedicated to enhancing your taspeeh experience. We are passionate about providing a seamless and enriching environment for individuals seeking spiritual connection through the practice of taspeeh. Our website is the brainchild of Sadiiq Mukhtaar Maxamed, who envisioned a digital space where users could find resources, guidance, and a community to support their taspeeh journey.


Who We Are:

At Taspeeh Keeper, we are a team of dedicated individuals committed to fostering a deeper understanding and appreciation for taspeeh. Our platform serves as a virtual sanctuary for those who wish to explore, learn, and connect with others who share a similar spiritual path.


Founder's Vision: Sadiiq Mukhtaar Maxamed, the visionary behind Taspeeh Keeper, recognized the need for a comprehensive online platform that caters to the diverse needs of individuals practicing taspeeh. His commitment to creating a space that goes beyond a mere collection of beads led to the development of this website. Sadiiq believes that taspeeh is not just a routine but a profound spiritual experience that can positively impact lives.


What Sets Us Apart:

1: Educational Resources:
We provide informative articles, tutorials, and guides to help users deepen their understanding of taspeeh, its history, and its significance.
2: Interactive Taspeeh Tools: Our website features interactive taspeeh tools designed to enhance your taspeeh sessions. From customizable digital tasbeeh counters to guided audio sessions, we offer a variety of resources to suit your preferences.
3: Community Hub:
Connect with like-minded individuals in our community hub. Share your experiences, seek advice, and build connections with people who understand the significance of taspeeh in their lives.
4: Events and Workshops:
Taspeeh Keeper organizes virtual events and workshops, bringing experts and enthusiasts together to share insights, tips, and stories related to taspeeh.


Get in Touch:
Have questions, suggestions, or just want to reach out? Sadiiq Mukhtaar Maxamed welcomes your feedback and inquiries. Feel free to contact him via email at sadiiqmukhtaar@gmail.com. Join us at Taspeeh Keeper and embark on a transformative journey as you explore the spiritual depth of taspeeh. Together, let's weave a tapestry of connection and mindfulness through this ancient and meaningful practice.
